Personal Thoughts

June 7th, 2014

These past four years have been wonderful, and I leave the high school with no regrets. You are lucky to go to El Segundo High, if you hate this wonderful high school, then I advise you to take part in more activities, be open to other viewpoints, don't be scared of having fun. High school isn't about being perfect, it's about finding who YOU are, and exenuating yourself to see how successful you can be among diverse crowds. Cliques do not exist if you forget about them, perfect grades do not matter if you understand the value of education, and haters do not matter if you can love who you are and stay confident with your decisions. I am proud of how I've grown as a person, all it took was opening my mind. My one big piece of advice: Don't ever demand gratitude or apologies, demand yourself. Thank you everyone for having an influence on me in some way, small or large. You are truly wonderful, and I wish you all the best in your future ventures.
