About Me

As a California native with a determined mindset, embracing the idea of "work hard, play hard" comes naturally to me. I am a recent graduate of El Segundo High School, and will be attending Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo starting September 2014. I am a strong proponent of self-learning through initiative and experience. To encourage that ideal I have tutored elementary through college students, mentored for the Roller Coaster Project, and mentored middle school students in VEX robotics.

When I'm done being a student/teacher for the day, you can find me napping, driving around LA with my friends, messing around in the robotics lab, practicing trombone (rare though), checking my Facebook/Twitter/Email, or raiding my friends' pools. I manage to keep myself busy with something though, so the amount of free time I get can vary from none to a little week to week. I guess you could say Productivity is my best friend.
